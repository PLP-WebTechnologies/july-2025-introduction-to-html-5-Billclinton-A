Nutrition

Eating a balanced diet is essential for overall health. Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your daily meals.

Exercise

Regular physical activity helps maintain a healthy weight, boosts mood, and improves cardiovascular health. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise most days.

Mental Health

Taking care of your mental well-being is as important as physical health. Practice mindfulness, maintain social connections, and get adequate rest.
